Frequently Asked Questions

Will 'Epicurean Reserve' provide the ingredients for the dishes?

We will give you the list of ingredients required. However, we don't provide the ingredients.

For how many people can I book Chef for Event?

You can book Chef for Party for upto 60 adults and 60 children.

Do I get to try the cook before taking the subscription?

Yes, we offer a paid trial service. Trial fee will be adjusted to your final subscription cost. Upon booking a trial, we'll assign a cook as per your requirements. If you are satisfied with the service, the same cook will be assigned for your monthly subscription. If not satisfied, we'll arrange a second trial with another cook.

What if I am not satisfied with my cook's service?

We offer one free cook replacement in case you are dissatisfied with your cook's service.

Will my cook clean the utensils?

Utensil cleaning is not included in the subscription. The cook will only prepare your meals and clean the slab and stove.

Where is this service available?

Epicurean Reserve is servieable in Solapur, Pune and Sangli

Is Epicurian Reserve Hiring cook's?

We often hire chef's. For seeking job opportunities mail us your CV on epicurean.job@gmail.com
